Heavy Machinery Operations Guide

This guide covers essential information about excavators, bulldozers, and cranes.

Excavators:
- Operating weight: 15,000-90,000 kg
- Engine power: 100-400 HP
- Bucket capacity: 0.5-3.0 cubic meters
- Maximum digging depth: 6-10 meters

Bulldozers:
- Engine: 200-800 HP turbocharged diesel
- Blade width: 3-5 meters
- Blade capacity: 4-20 cubic meters
- Ground pressure: 10-15 PSI

Safety Requirements:
- Daily pre-operation inspection
- Proper training certification required
- Use of PPE (hard hat, steel-toed boots)
- Clear communication with ground personnel
